My current educational background

After graduating with a Bac specialised in Maths and Physics-Chemistry, I am currently studying for a BUT Networks & Telecoms degree. This university bachelor of technology allows students to acquire professional and theoretical knowledge in the field of computer networks and telecommunications in three years.

The subjects covered in this field include computer network configurations, security, data flow management, information transport, cybersecurity, network protocols, computer security.

This training allows us to respond to the challenges of our time by training technicians capable of intervening in these areas of competence. From the second year onwards, I'll chose the Cybersecurity programme

From the second year onwards, I'm opting for the Cybersecurity course to develop my skills in IT security policies.

My future project

The Cybersecurity programme integrated into the BUT Networks and Telecoms will enable me to acquire theoretical and practical knowledge in this particular field. I will be trained in the risks of attacks on computer systems and will be able to analyse and manage a security policy in a professional structure. Further learning such as the protection of sensitive data and the application of European cyber defence laws (RGPD) will also be studied.

According to the new possibilities of parallel admission between BUT and engineering school, I would like to pursue a Cybersecurity course in the 3rd or 4th year of studies by joining the ENSIBB engineering school. This complete training (BUT + engineering diploma) will allow me to apply the skills I have acquired throughout my schooling to meet the challenges of cybersecurity, particularly in terms of defence against computer attacks and the protection of sensitive data.
